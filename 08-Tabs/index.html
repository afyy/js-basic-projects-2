about

Traditional Cappadocian houses and dovecotes carved into caves reflect the uniqueness of the region. These houses have been constructed on hillsides by using rocks mainly in the 19th century.

history

Cappadocia region was formed 60 million years ago by erosion of soft layers of lava and ash from Mount Erciyes (Argeus), Mount Hasan and Mount Güllü composing with wind and rain over millions of years.

vision

Our vision is to increase the number of tourists coming to Cappadocia every year. In this way, significantly increasing the amount of cash entering the country and the city.

goals

Our main goal is to restore the sights of the city and attract more tourists. For this reason, our aim is to advertise for the development of every touristic place in Nevsehir.
